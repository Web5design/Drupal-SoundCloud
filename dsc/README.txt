Drupal SoundCloud.

- Associates a SoundCloud account (http://soundcloud.com) with a Drupal user's
account.
- Interfaces with the SoundCloud API to provide Drupal site with information
about user's SoundCloud music tracks.
- Render's tracks in anunordered list of SoundCloud track URLs for Drupal
SoundCloud Player to turn into an in-page playlist and streaming player.

- Exposes SoundCloud data to views for customizable SoundCloud tracklist and
playlist generation.
